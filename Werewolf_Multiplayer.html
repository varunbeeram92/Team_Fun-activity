🐺 Werewolf

12-PLAYER MULTIPLAYER + AI

A real-time game of trust, lies, and survival — play with your team.

🌙 The story: A peaceful village hides a deadly secret. Each night, three werewolves quietly slip away to hunt a victim. By day, the survivors gather in the square to argue, accuse, and vote out whoever they believe is a wolf in disguise.

The catch: the wolves look exactly like everyone else, and they're sitting right there in the discussion — lying to your face. The village must figure out who they are before the pack outnumbers them. Empty seats are filled by AI players who chat, scheme, and vote just like humans, so you won't always know who's real.
🎭 The 12 roles in every game:

Village team 🏘️ (wins by eliminating all wolves)

🔮
Detective×1
Each night, secretly investigates one player and learns whether they are a werewolf. Your most powerful ally — if they survive.
💊
Doctor×1
Each night, protects one player from the wolves' attack. Can't protect the same person two nights in a row.
🏹
Hunter×1
A trained marksman. The moment the Hunter dies — killed at night or voted out by day — they fire one last shot and take any player of their choice down with them. A wolf who kills the Hunter might lose a packmate instantly.
🏘️
Villagers×7
No special powers, but their votes decide who lives or dies. Their weapon is discussion, logic, and reading people.

Werewolf team 🐺 (wins when wolves equal the villagers)

🐺
Werewolves×3
Each night they secretly agree on one victim to kill. By day they pose as innocent villagers, deflecting blame and steering votes toward the innocent.
📋 How a round flows:
  • Pick your character and join a room — every game has 12 players, with AI filling any empty seats.
  • Roles are dealt out secretly and at random — only you know your own role.
  • 🌙 Night: the village "sleeps." Wolves choose a victim, the Detective investigates, the Doctor protects.
  • ☀️ Day (60s): the night's outcome is revealed, then everyone discusses — accuse, defend, bluff, and form alliances.
  • 🗳️ Vote (45s): the village votes. A majority eliminates that player (and reveals their role), or choose No Lynch to spare everyone.
  • Night and day repeat until one side wins. 🐺 Wolves win when they equal the villagers; 🏘️ the village wins when every wolf is gone.
  • 💡 Tip: dead players become spectators and can chat with other ghosts — but the living can't hear them.
