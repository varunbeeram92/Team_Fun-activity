Type the displayed text as fast and accurately as you can!

📋 How it works:
  • 10 rounds — sentences get longer as you progress
  • Time increases with difficulty (15s → 60s)
  • Type what you see on screen — green = correct, red = wrong
  • Earn bonus points for finishing early ⚡ and perfect accuracy 🎯
  • Highest total score across all 10 rounds wins! 🏆
📊 How is the score calculated? (click to expand)
📖 Scoring per round:
  • Base score = WPM × Accuracy %
  • ⚡ Speed bonus — finish the sentence before time runs out:
    Time remainingBonus
    More than 50% left+20 pts
    25% – 50% left+10 pts
  • 🎯 Perfect accuracy bonus — type the entire sentence with zero mistakes → +15 pts

📖 What do the stats mean?
  • WPM (Words Per Minute) — how fast you type. Every 5 correct characters = 1 word. Average person types 40–60 WPM.
  • Accuracy — percentage of characters you typed correctly out of total characters typed.
  • Avg Accuracy — your accuracy averaged across all 10 rounds.
